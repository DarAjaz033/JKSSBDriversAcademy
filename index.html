Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers

What is included in the Complete Package?
The Complete Package includes all three parts (Traffic Rules, MV Act, and Mechanical Knowledge) with 2500+ MCQs, video lectures for every topic, 15 full-length mock tests, PDF study material, and a free MV Act book. You also get lifetime access to all content and 24/7 support.
How long do I have access to the course?
Individual parts (Part I, II, or III) give you 6 months of access. The Complete Package offers lifetime access, meaning you can access all content forever with all future updates included at no extra cost.
Can I access courses on my mobile phone?
Yes! Our platform is fully mobile-friendly and works on all devices. You can watch videos, practice MCQs, and take mock tests on your smartphone, tablet, or computer. Offline access is also available for videos and study material.
Are the mock tests similar to actual JKSSB exam?
Yes, our mock tests are designed exactly like the actual JKSSB driver exam with the same pattern, difficulty level, and time duration. They include 100 MCQs to be solved in 90 minutes, just like the real exam.
What if I need help during preparation?
We provide 24/7 doubt support through chat and email. Our expert faculty will answer all your questions and help you clear your doubts. You can reach us anytime through the Contact Us section.
Is the content updated regularly?
Yes, we regularly update our content based on the latest JKSSB notifications, exam pattern changes, and feedback from students who recently appeared for the exam. All updates are free for enrolled students.
